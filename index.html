Introducing the Newest App for Water Lovers

We've all been there. It's extremely hot outside, you're thirsty, and spending a dollar on 16 ounces of water just doesn't feel right. That's where Aqua Scout comes to help. You can search near by for drinking fountains. No more guessing and no more running around. Aqua Scout takes the guessing game out of the equation.

User Generated Content

See a water fountain not on the list? Add it! Our list of water fountains are all generated by user reviews! The more fountains you add the more we can help people get healthy and save money.

Advanced Searching

Need a water fountain quick? Narrow your choices down to 1 mile. Jogging and might need water later? Broaden your search to 3 miles! You can search by address or use your built in GPS to automatically find the nearest drinking fountains.

Try it Today!

Download and try our app today. The bigger our community gets the more we can help everyone. Be a part of it!
